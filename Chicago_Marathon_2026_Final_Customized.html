Key Workout Notes
Strides (●) — 6–8 × 100 m accelerations tacked onto the end of easy runs. Controlled build to 5-K effort with full recovery. Keeps legs sharp without meaningful fatigue.
Wednesday Workouts — Follow the Mill City Running Fall Marathon training plan for the week's primary quality session.
MP Long Runs (highlighted blue) — Easy-pace miles bookending the marked MP segment. Jun 19: 8@MP · Jul 11: 10@MP · Aug 8 (Sat, Wk 9): 12@MP · Sep 5: 14@MP. Runs at 7:41–8:21 outside the MP window.
Taper (3 Weeks) — Volume drops sharply; intensity stays. One workout + one moderate long run each week through Week 1. The hay is in the barn.
